Project > Adaptation of old casino's premises into a new retail unit

We have worked with a variety of clients in Poland, including some major European brands like House and Deutsche Bank on projects ranging from the re-branding and refurbishment of an existing retail chain to the creative design of a new store format and the subsequent roll-out of that format across multiple locations.

Case studies:

  • Adaptation of old casino's premises into a new retail unit
  • A retail unit in a new built shopping centre
  • A high street franchise outlet
  • Project management

House is a clothing brand which currently has 290 stores in 11 European countries. This project called for the transformation of a small space within a former casino into a retail space suitable for a boutique. Although not listed, this 19th century building is located in a heart of conservation area within a UNESCO World Heritage Site and as such required a very respectful design approach on all project stages. The long list of strict requirements from the city's conservator, local building control and the building's inhabitants had to be met both during design and construction processes.
