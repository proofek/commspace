Welcome to Commspace!

Commspace are UK commercial architects who specialise in architectural, interior and fit-out design within Poland. We work with international brands that are looking to establish a presence in Poland or expand their existing operations within the country.

We help our clients to translate their vision into reality and to ensure their commercial space is aligned with their brand and corporate strategy.

We work with clients from a variety of sectors including Retail, Leisure, Finance and Food and Drink.

Our typical client is multi-site, international and high growth, looking to enter or expand their presence in the Polish market. We work on projects of varying scope and size, ranging from a single high street retail unit to a nationwide expansion project.

We have the capability to handle complex and accelerated roll outs, working independently, with a selected group of specialist consultants or with the client's in-house team.
