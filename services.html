Services

Commspace works with our clients on projects ranging from a single location to a nationwide roll out. Key areas of expertise include architecture, interior and fit-out design for various market sectors including retail, leisure, banking, office development, restaurant and bars.

We are uniquely positioned to help our clients succeed in the Polish market as a result of:

  • Our Polish links with building contractors, specialist consultants, and manufacturers, and our knowledge and experience of the Polish marketplace
  • Experience within the UK design and construction market working with major UK and international brands
  • Technical and creative skills developed from 60 client engagements in the UK and Poland across multiple sectors

Our services fall into three key areas:

  1. Design: Architectural, Interior and Fit-out design
  2. Improve: Re-branding and Refurbishment
  3. Manage: Project Management
