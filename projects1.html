House is a clothing brand which currently has 290 stores in 11 European countries. This project called for the transformation of a small space within a former casino into a retail space suitable for a boutique. Although not listed, this 19th century building is located in a heart of conservation area within a UNESCO World Heritage Site and as such required a very respectful design approach on all project stages. The long list of strict requirements from the city's conservator, local building control and the building's inhabitants had to be met both during design and construction processes.The successful space planning was achieved through the demolition of internal load bearing walls on the ground floor level which allowed the separated spaces to be opened up and interlinked in order to maximise the sales area. In addition to the complex internal fit-out works, the programme also included the renovation of the facade, the replacement of all external windows, the re-positioning of an existing cash machine and the installation of new signage. The project was successfully completed on time and within budget, meeting challenging time lines which were necessitated due to the start of the peak shopping season.
